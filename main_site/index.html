About

Research at the intersection of economics and AI

KAIST EconAI Lab develops and applies computational methods to understand economic activity, organizational behavior, public policy, and social outcomes. The lab connects rigorous empirical research with emerging AI systems and large-scale data sources.

Research Areas

What We Study

The lab's work is organized around AI-enabled economic measurement, prediction, and decision support.

AI for Economic Analysis

Machine learning and causal empirical methods for studying firms, markets, technology adoption, and economic behavior.

Satellite and Spatial Data

Remote sensing, geospatial signals, and alternative data for measuring local economic activity and social change.

LLMs and Social Systems

Evaluation and application of language models in research workflows, organizations, and decision environments.
